The Hybryde team

The Hybryde team was made up of passionate amateurs; the work was based primarily on observation and especially on the remarks and suggestions of members of the various forums we frequented.
We had only one guiding principle: To move forward with respect for Hybryde users and also to allow everyone to see their work integrated into our distributions.
Hybryde works in a spirit of passion and sharing, It was a platform to demonstrate the work of talented coders and graphic designers of all ages and nationalities.

  • We parted ways in 2012, and it is a joy for me to offer you this 2026 version.

  • Hybryde is a unique concept that will be a hit at Linux demo parties.
